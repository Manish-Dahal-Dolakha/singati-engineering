About Our Company

Building excellence since 2010

Leading Engineering & Construction Firm

Singati Engineering Pvt Ltd is a premier construction and engineering company dedicated to delivering high-quality infrastructure projects. With over a decade of experience, we have successfully completed numerous projects across Nepal, earning a reputation for reliability, innovation, and timely delivery.

Our team of experienced engineers, architects, and project managers work collaboratively to transform client visions into reality while adhering to the highest standards of safety and sustainability.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional engineering solutions that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be Nepal's most trusted and innovative engineering firm, recognized for transforming infrastructure and contributing to sustainable development.

Our Values

Integrity, quality, innovation, safety, and client satisfaction are the core principles that guide every project we undertake.
